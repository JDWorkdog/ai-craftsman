# Research

Conduct comprehensive research on any topic with intelligent source selection and professional output.

## What It Does

This prompt helps you conduct thorough research with:
- Intelligent source selection (web vs. knowledge base)
- Multiple depth levels (quick, standard, deep)
- Flexible output formats
- Proper citation and source tracking
- Quality verification across sources

## When to Use

- Market research and competitive analysis
- Industry trends and emerging topics
- Technical deep dives and comparisons
- Background research before decisions
- Fact-finding for presentations or documents

## Key Features

- **Adaptive sourcing**: Uses web search for current events, knowledge base for established topics
- **Multiple depth levels**: Quick (5-10 min) to deep (30+ min) research
- **Flexible formats**: Executive summary, detailed report, Q&A, comparative analysis
- **Source quality**: Prioritizes authoritative, credible sources
- **Uncertainty handling**: Notes areas of consensus, debate, and information gaps

## Output Structure

Generated research includes:
- **Executive Summary**: 2-4 paragraphs with key findings
- **Detailed Report**: Organized sections based on topic nature
- **Sources**: Properly cited with links/references
- **Key Insights**: Most significant findings highlighted
- **Considerations**: Limitations and areas needing further research
